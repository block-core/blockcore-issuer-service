What is a decentralized identifier (DID)?

A unique identity that belongs to you. There are many providers of DIDs and you are free to choose anyone you want.

You can get a DID with wallets such as:

  • Verida Wallet
  • Sphereon Wallet

If you have a wallet like MetaMask (Ethereum) or Stellar, you can fill out your identity like these examples:

did:ethr:0x17960Abb12a3C...
did:stllr:GDMBM7P2ZVD64D...
